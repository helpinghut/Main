    INDEPENDENCE DAY CELEBRATION

    We celebrate Independence Day in our country to remind us that our country and its freedom is the result of sweat, patience, persistence, and sacrifice of those with the courage to dream of freedom and make it a reality for their future generations. The Indian freedom struggle and its seeds were cultivated long ago with the beginning of the education phase of our freedom fighters. Proper education imparts knowledge and develops awareness, communication and various skills. Keeping that spirit in mind, HELPING HUT celebrated Independence Day on August 15th 2020 by distributing education kits to children who cannot bear the cost of education. These children with free-spirit and open minds participated enthusiastically in the celebration by not only drawing the national flag but also by taking their first steps towards their education. These flags not only represented our sense of freedom but also a sense of service and responsibility that comes along with it.

    TEACHER'S DAY CELEBRATION

    On the occasion of teacher's day with the cooperation of coordinators of Helping Hut in collaboration with Mr. Pramod Kumar, Assistant Professor at University of Delhi, conducted an awareness session on "Importance of studies on children's life and being independent especially for rural children ''. The session focused on uplifting the minds of children to help them clearly understand the motive of life and the need of education to drive them into the world of opportunities and technology.The mentor guidance and affection will help them discover their own path in the vast variety of options in life.     DIWALI CELEBRATION

    With a motive to eradicate the sufferings of our society , we, the members of Helping Hut, took a pledge to cause least harm to the environment and help as many people as possible. Firstly, our aim is to spread awareness among people to cause less pollution and instead hold a society fest which would include a grand party and a series of events involving discussion games as in, this way people come together and build strong bonds . We enjoy and have amazing fun with the people around. We have conducted this fest in various societies across Dwarka and Nazafgarh numerous times. Last diwali, we heard the happiness of people, and their laughter instead of the usual firecrackers that we were accustomed to, every year. In order to help the small shopkeepers we encourage people to buy their products and inform them that their products are better if not as good as big brands .They are also cheaper and help their families to celebrate a joyous Diwali.

    CHILDREN’S DAY CELEBRATION

    Nehru Ji had a glorious love for children and considered that children are the upcoming future creators of the nation. If we need to preserve our future, then it is the duty of all of us to build the future of these children in a better direction. In a glimpse of his love for children, our land began to remember his birthday as Children’s Day. The main purpose of this celebration was to make all the citizens of India aware of giving rights to children to get education. Keeping that in mind we celebrated children’s day with utmost pleasure with kids who didn’t have much privilege and became their wind beneath their wings. It was really great to see those happy faces.
