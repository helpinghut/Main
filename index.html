    UDAAN STREET SCHOOL


  • Every child should have the opportunity to recieve a quality education


  • "Education is for improving the lives of others and for leaving your community & world better than you found it." With this in mind Helping Hut started an initiative a few months back of UDAAN STREET SCHOOL and are already teaching 50+ underprivileged children & hence, making their life much better & fulfilled. You can also start this at your place, providing education to underprivileged children who don't have access to quality education!!

  • Therefore, Helping Hut NGO provides free education at ' udaan - Street School ' at
  • Sec 13 near Dwarka, New Delhi
  • Sec 14 near Dwarka, New Delhi
  • Near Vegas Mall, New Delhi



    • BEGGAR TRANSFORMATION

    HELPING HUT helped a beggar from Dwarka ,who begged for years to survive , by providing him with a sewing machine so that he can live his life with full dignity. Likewise we also provide skills to other beggars to transform their life for a brighter future.
