    COMPETITIONS

    Good Food, Good Mood

    Food competition is held by helping hut NGOs for making their skill of cooking and more appreciated. We all have fun and make that day memorial.

    We provided them all they needed things to make the food like chopped vegetables, sandwich bread, spices and Condiments to help them unleash their creativity. Their food was tasted, judged and awarded. At the end of the day, everybody had fun and we made this day memorable.

    With this competition, we make their skills rewarded and make them capable to take participate in big competition in the outer world.

    Art is not what the artist sees but what one can make others see.

    Diving into the world of imagination of our young amateurs, Helping hut NGO conducted various drawing competitions on different occasions. We conducted drawing and painting workshops to assist the kids in realising their hidden artistic talents and provided them with materials to demonstrate their creativity.

    We have held numerous workshops in the past, such as on independence day, when youngsters with a free spirit and an open mind eagerly participated in the celebration by not only drawing the national flag but also taking their first step towards education. These flags signified not just our sense of liberty, but also the sense of duty and responsibility associated with it.
