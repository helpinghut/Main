    Campus Ambassador

    College Representatives/ Campus Ambassasors:

    Responsibilities:

    The Campus Ambassador's day-to-day responsibilities include:

    1. The first task will be to form a team within your college.
    2. Core team members need to be appointed.
    3. Volunteers need to be hired within your team.
    4. Online and Offline - work depending on comfortability and requirements.
    5. You need to be ready for every challenge and opportunity.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Have excellent Leadership skills
    2. Committed and loyalty towards the ngo
    3. Building strong connections within the college
    4. Need to work closely working with Founder and Core Team
    5. Arrange regular meets in college with the volunteers as well as the core members

    Internship's

    Fundraising Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Creating awareness about the social cause.
    2. Raising funds for noble cause (either through their network or by using social media).
    3. Daily report of their workings to the concerned head.
    4. Minimum hours to be devoted daily in order to achieve the goals.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Business Communication
    2. Social Media Maketing
    3. Convincing capabilities
    4. Good network with people

    Web development Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Making websites on WordPress.
    2. Working on SEO and digital marketing.
    3. Developing frontend and backend.
    4. Translating designs and wireframes into high quality and pixels perfect code.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Should have intermediate knowledge and skillset on Wordpress, html, javascript, MySQL
    2. Should have excellent communication skills
    3. Should have the willingness to work around the clock as per requirement
    4. Learning attitude
    5. Should have worked on website or currently working

    Public Relations Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Work on collaboration with influencers/media pages/organizations/magazines/newspapers.
    2. Own mastery on social media platforms.
    3. Create and curate engaging content.
    4. Communicate and build relationships with current or prospective clients.
    5. Effectively utilize the organization's social media and blogs.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Social Media Marketing
    2. Digital Marketing
    3. Communication Skills

    Teaching Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Taking online/offline classes for students associated with Udaan Street School. All these students are from low income or Govt. schools.
    2. Deciding on the topics to be taught to the students in accordance with their curriculum.
    3. Taking minimum of 3 classes per week to the maximum being 6.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Hindi Proficiency (Spoken & Written)
    2. English Proficiency (Spoken & Written)
    3. Mathematics, Social studies, Computer and basics of other subjects as well

    Video Editing Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Working on video editing tasks in connection with social media platforms.
    2. Creating video content with the help of original shooted video/images, etc.
    3. Inserting music (background music and SFX) and effects needed.
    4. Learning these skills with various resources.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Knowledge of Premiere Pro, Da Vinci Resolve, or any other professional software like Canva and After Effects (optional).
    2. Should have intermediate knowledge and skillset.
    3. Should have excellent communication skills.
    4. Should have the willingness to work around the clock as per requirement.
    5. Should have quick editing skills.

    Social Media Interns:

    Responsibilities:

    Day-to-day responsibilities of selected interns include:

    1. Brainstorming ideas and new ways to enhance the social media reach of our organization in Instagram, Facebook, Youtube and Twitter.
    2. Collaborating with other NGOs, influencers, and organizations.
    3. Widespreading the reach of organizations in different states and cities covering all over India.
    4. Taking part actively in all the tasks assigned to you, and ensuring the completion of the same within the given time limit.
    5. Presenting your own strategies and ideas through which we can help out more and more un-privileged people.

    Skill sets required:

    1. Computer Networking
    2. Digital Marketing
    3. Email Marketing
    4. English Proficiency (Written)
    5. Social Media Marketing(Facebook/Instagram/Youtube/Twitter)
    6. Project Management
    7. Research and Analytics
