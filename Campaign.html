    POLLUTION DAY AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

    Due to rapid industrialization , automobiles etc. pollution has increased to a great extent that it hampers our health and harms mother nature. Water pollution, air pollution and noise pollution are the most common types that we see and they have become so familiar that we ignore the fact that we are getting sick under the guise of development. Seeing the situation on the national pollution control day "HELPING HUT '' organized a digital campaign for creating awareness by means of videos and posters made by kids.It is rightly said that "Environmental pollution is an incurable disease. It can only be prevented"

    KEEP WATER SAVE LIFE

    When we step out in the summer we get drenched with sweat and feel extremely thirsty after having a glass of water we feel better. Same goes with the birds as well even they feel thirsty and hot during the summers. So,HELPING HUT came up with a campaign called "keep water save life" with vision that no bird should die and be affected in summer due to water scarcity. We asked our volunteers to keep a pot filled with water in the places where birds can easily find water like near the balconies , elevated area , parks etc.

    UTTHAAN ABHIYAAN

    The first step toward change is awareness, and the second is acceptance. Utthaan Abhiyaan initiated by the team of helping hut is one of the few initiatives that talk about spreading awareness about the policies that our Indian Government has taken for the underprivileged people of our society. This awareness campaign aimed at making people aware about government schemes that were or are being initiated to help them but they remain an alien concept to them. Our team unanimously visited the rural areas in order to ensure that correct information was spread. And as Phil Mcgraw once said, “Awareness without action is worthless”, we ensured that the people availed the benefits of the policies by telling them if they were eligible to avail those facilities or not, and also by informing them about the application processes.

    EDUCATION AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

    The higher the level of education, the less likely the person is to fall below the poverty line. This is because education imparts skills and knowledge associated with higher wages. Educating girls in particular makes them more likely to take control of decisions relating to fertility, family welfare, and health. HELPING HUT, TEAM DWARKA went down the streets of Dwarka to make people aware about the importance of education on 19th December 2021.education is the best way in which we can contribute to the nation's growth.
