    CAMPAIGNS

    POLLUTION DAY AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

    Due to rapid industrialization , automobiles etc. pollution has increased to a great extent that it hampers our health and harms mother nature. Water pollution, air pollution and noise pollution are the most common types that we see and they have become ... so familiar that we ignore the fact that we are getting sick under the guise of development. Seeing the situation on the national pollution control day "HELPING HUT '' organized a digital campaign for creating awareness by means of videos and posters made by kids.It is rightly said that "Environmental pollution is an incurable disease. It can only be prevented"

    KEEP WATER SAVE LIFE

    When we step out in the summer we get drenched with sweat and feel extremely thirsty after having a glass of water we feel better. Same goes with the birds as well even they feel thirsty and hot during the summers. So,HELPING HUT ... came up with a campaign called "keep water save life" with vision that no bird should die and be affected in summer due to water scarcity. We asked our volunteers to keep a pot filled with water in the places where birds can easily find water like near the balconies , elevated area , parks etc.

    “Cleanliness is a mindset – a positive habit that keeps the body, mind, and environment happy, healthy, simple, neat, and delightful.” - Amit Ray Cleanliness habits in many parts of India are extremely poor whether it be poor sanitation habits or people littering on the roads. ...     EDUCATION AWARENESS CAMPAIGN
