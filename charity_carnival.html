    CHARITY CARNIVAL

    Firstly what is charity carnival? Any event whose primary purpose is to generate funds for a cause, charity, or non-profit is considered a charity event. We are always trying to find causes to make ourselves happy, why not to make others happy without any interest or profit behind it? Well, that's what our organization did in December, 2021. We gave a shot ,to make little one's out there happy and took the heavy burden of responsibility from their shoulders and let them freely enjoy their childhood just for a while. We organised number of games like find the coin in the sand , treasure hunt , skipping race, blow the candles, and so on .Also assigned gifts with it such as geometry box, lunchbox, stationary items, refreshments and so on .Food stalls such as sandwich, bhel puri, fresh salad, beverages etc free playing and interacting space and n number of other things. we were focused on homeless children's education but in addition to it their physical health too matter the same for us. The joy in the eyes of little munchkin while playing those games was WORTH it. It was our little steps towards bringing big smile on those small faces which for sure gonna turn into big steps in future. We were very delightful to be part of this amazing event with such beautiful children and kind hearted subordinates.
